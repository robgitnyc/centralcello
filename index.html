Central Cello (live in New York)

Central Cello was founded by Roberto Matus, Chilean cellist born south of Bio Bio river. Currently living in New York City, Central Cello brings you the broadest repertoire made of sonatas for cello and piano, suites for cello solo (such as those composed by J. S. Bach), and also incredible arrangements of songs of the popular culture repertoire, such as movie classics, jazz standards and jazz classics, Rock hits of all time, and much more.

This website is currently in expansion. If you want to get in touch with Central Cello, you can write to robcellonyc@gmail.com or call/text (615)981-9534.
